Crane Assisted Removal

The use of cranes is the industry standard for the removal of trees from difficult and hard to reach areas, allowing maximum climber safety. Cranes lift and carry the heavy pieces over large structures to a staging area with minimal impact to lawns, plantings or nearby structures. Contact us today!

Cabling and Bracing

There are two types of support cabling, Dynamic & Static. Dynamic cabling consists of a heavy load bearing fabric sling with end loops connecting the two limbs together. Static cabling consists of either a steel braided cable, or a stainless steel rod drilled through the two limbs and fixed with terminal ends.

Insect Control

Tree insect problems are common in our area. Insects don’t disease trees, they create pathways allowing pathogens like fungi into the tree. They often concentrate in one area, leaving your tree extremely vulnerable to structural failure in high wind or similar situations. Contact us for a free quote today!

Learn More

Disease and Decay

Diseased trees should be monitored annually. They are more susceptible to normal environmental conditions. Aside from the obvious lack of vigor that you are able to see, there could be serious heartwood decay or voids inside the tree that are undetectable. Let us assess your tree's health!

Pruning

Your Arborlife professional can determine what type of pruning is necessary to maintain or improve the health, safety, and appearance of your trees. This may include removing limbs that interfere with houses, removing hazardous limbs, or improving the shape of your tree.
